About Dr. Niyanta

Welcome to the serene sanctuary of Holistic Homeo Healing, where Dr. Niyanta's healing legacy, as a second-generation homeopath following in the esteemed footsteps of her grandmother, unfolds like a captivating tale of compassion and expertise. With a rich tapestry woven from the strands of academic prowess and empathetic care, Dr. Niyanta's journey stands as a testament to her unwavering dedication to the art of healing.

Armed with a Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS) from the renowned Lord Mahavira Homeopathic Medical College & Hospital, Ludhiana, and fortified by a Post Graduate Diploma in Counseling Psychology (PGDCP) from the prestigious Amity University, Noida, Dr. Niyanta's educational repertoire is both comprehensive and distinguished.

However, it is her myriad of accomplishments that truly distinguishes her. From skillfully addressing the formidable challenges posed by conditions such as kidney stones, gallstones, alopecia, and jaundice within remarkably brief timeframes, to her compassionate service during her tenure as a school counselor and subsequently as a counselor at a De-addiction center, Dr. Niyanta's achievements are as diverse as they are impressive.

Yet, it is not merely the accolades that adorn her path; it is the harmony of skills she possesses that truly illuminates her journey. Dr. Niyanta adeptly combines traditional wisdom with modern insights, crafting bespoke treatment plans that not only heal the body but also nourish the soul. Her compassionate approach, rooted in empathy and understanding, ensures that each patient receives not only medical care but also compassionate guidance on their journey to wellness.

Step into the realm of Holistic Homeo Healing, where Dr. Niyanta's expertise awaits, poised to paint a masterpiece of health, happiness, and harmony in your life.
